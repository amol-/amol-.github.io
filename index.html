Welcome

I'm a Software Engineer and father of two, based in Torino, Italy.

I have been leading software development teams for 20+ years and Software Development surprisingly remains my main passion.

I currently work at Posit where I help creating impactful projects for the Python ecosystem, and I previously was the Senior Director of Open Source Engineering at Voltron Data, where I coordinated contributions and maintenance of core data engineering projects for Data Analytics like Apache Arrow, Ibis and Substrait

I have also been a founder of AXANT.it Python company, a shareholder of Mamazen Startup Studio and a member of the IH1 investment holding.

I'm also deeply passionate about Python Software Development as an, author of Python related books, speaker at multiple conferences around europe, guest at multiple online shows on Software Engineering, and maintainer of several OpenSource projects

I also mix my interest for data engineering and Python in the Monthly Python Data Engineering newsletter
